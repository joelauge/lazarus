Our Story

Founded in 1972, Lazarus began as a small engineering firm with a big vision: to revolutionize how industries handle belt systems. What started as a passion for precision engineering has evolved into a global force driving industrial automation.

Today, we're not just manufacturers - we're innovators, problem-solvers, and partners in progress. Every system we build carries the weight of five decades of expertise, refined through thousands of real-world applications.
